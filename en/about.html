About the Schwamm2 Association (SM2A)

Home Association Structure Application Rules

The Schwamm2 Association (SM2A) is an independent 7v7 football association providing a modern, fair, and clear competition structure. The project's goal is to offer clubs and players a structured alternative to traditional full-field football.

Under SM2A, national and continental associations organize their own leagues, cup competitions, and championships. The focus is on clear rules, simple organization, and active involvement of teams in competitions.

7v7 football stands for dynamism, enjoyment, and accessibility – for new and smaller clubs alike. SM2A grows gradually and openly, aiming to organize football in a new and independent way.

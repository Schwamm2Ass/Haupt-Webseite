Application Criteria – For All Associations

  • Who can apply?
    • Any club or organization that wants to play 7vs7 football.
    • Both newly founded and existing clubs can submit an application.
  • Application Procedure
    • The application must be submitted in writing (by email).
    • Required information in the application:
      • Club name
      • Location / City
      • Contact person (with details)
      • Number of players / squad size
      • Club colors / kits
      • Additional information can be optionally added.
  • Application Review
    • The SM2A checks the application for completeness and forwards it to the national association.
    • Applications are not rejected as long as minimum requirements are met.
  • Membership
    • After approval, the club becomes an official member of the national association.
    • Member clubs must follow the official rules of the SM2A and the continent.
  • Support for Clubs
    • Newly founded or smaller clubs receive organizational support (e.g., kits or training facilities).
    • Continental competition costs can be covered upon request.
    • Support applies only to the cases mentioned.
